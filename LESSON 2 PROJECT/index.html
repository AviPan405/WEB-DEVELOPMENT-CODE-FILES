Welcome to IES Helsingborg

IES Helsingborg is a secondary and high school (Years 4-9) located in Helsingborg, Sweden. We offer a variety of programs and courses to help students achieve their academic and personal goals. Our dedicated staff and modern facilities provide an excellent learning environment for all our students.

About Us

At IES Helsingborg, we believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive community where students can thrive. Our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire students, preparing them for higher education and future careers. We also offer a range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and clubs, to enhance the overall student experience.
