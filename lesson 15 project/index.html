Hobbies to try

Art

Art is the expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual, auditory, or performing form, to communicate ideas, emotions, or beauty. While its definition has evolved from mere "skill" or "craft" in the 17th century to focus on aesthetic and emotional power, art remains a diverse range of activities that transcend cultural and historical boundaries.

Sport

While there is no single universal definition, a sport is generally defined as a competitive physical activity or game that is played according to a fixed set of rules for enjoyment or as a professional job.

Music

As a hobby, music is the intentional engagement with sound for personal pleasure, self-expression, or social connection rather than professional gain. It is one of the most versatile hobbies because it can be purely internal (listening) or highly active (creating).
